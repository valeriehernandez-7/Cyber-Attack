Cyberattack

Created by Valerie Hernandez and released in 2021, CyberAttack is a survival and strategy game. Our planet's system is being attacked by alien cyber threats. You'll have to run a special ship designed to travel to this binary world. Your mission will be that as the invaders attempt to reach the entrance you must eliminate them with high-power attacks. But beware, because they have tactical formations of attack and you may face one of their bosses during the battle. You're our last line of defense, the planet trusts you, agent.

creative process

Project management

planning

The project planning stage required thorough diligence as it established the project roadmap. In this phase, the main tasks were to identify technical requirements, develop a detailed project schedule, create a communication plan, and establish goals and deliverables. 

Execution

The project execution stage was where the actual work was developed. As the project manager, I established efficient workflows and carefully monitor the progress of the work. This ensured that everything remained in order and that the project ran smoothly. 

control

This phase was executed simultaneously with the execution of the project, which ensured that the objectives and deliverables of the project were met. Dividing a project into multiple phases gives the project a semblance of predictability.
